Plan your dream vacation – Don't avoid your vacation

Hello, we are BlueStar, your free travel app. We know how stressful it can get when it comes to planning and organizing vacations. So, let us help you with your next travel.

Unlimited trip planning

Never stress out again! Our app is here to help you plan and organize all your travel needs. From solo trips to business trips to family trips.

Invite Friends and Families

You have the flexibility to create a trip and invite others to join you. You can plan trip with family members, with friends and even strangers (if you are feeling little adventurous).

Join a getaway

CDon’t feel like planning your trip, then, join others who are headed to the same destination during your travel dates.

Private or Public

You can keep your trips private and allow only those you have invited to view your trip. Or you can make your trip public, allowing others to join you.

How it works — Simple as 1, 2, 3

2

3
